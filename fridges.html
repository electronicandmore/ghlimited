Refrigerator Buying Guide

Choosing the right refrigerator for your home involves considering several factors. Here are some key points to help you make an informed decision:

Type & Size

Consider the space available and your family size. Single-door models are compact, while side-by-side and multi-door models offer more storage but require more space.

Capacity

For 1-2 people, 100-300 liters is often sufficient. For families of 3-4, consider 300-500 liters, and for larger families, look for 500+ liters.

Energy Efficiency

Look for refrigerators with high energy star ratings to reduce electricity consumption and save on bills over time.

Features

Consider features like frost-free operation, adjustable shelves, water & ice dispensers, and smart connectivity based on your needs.
