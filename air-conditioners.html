Air Conditioner Buying Guide

Choosing the right air conditioner for your space requires considering several important factors:

BTU Rating

The British Thermal Unit (BTU) measurement indicates cooling capacity. As a rule of thumb, you need about 20 BTU per square foot of living space for efficient cooling.

AC Type

Split ACs are quiet and efficient but require installation. Window units are more affordable and easier to install. Portable ACs offer flexibility but are less efficient.

Energy Efficiency

Look for inverter technology and high energy efficiency rating (EER) to reduce power consumption and save on electricity bills over time.

Features

Consider additional features like air purification, dehumidification, smart controls, timer functions, sleep mode, and remote operation capabilities.
